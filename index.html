About HUNG

I'm a Computer Science Major at UC Irvine, class of 2021. I had led multiple collaborative projects, some of which you can find above. My experience includes working as a Producer, Game Designer, Level Designer, Programmer, and Artist.

Colonial 'Murica

I developed Colonial 'Murica in 2016 as a final project for my high school civics class. It was my very first exposure to the Unity engine. With exception to audio, every aspect of this game was entirely developed by me. My teacher never got past the boat segment, and gave up after a couple of tries. I got an A for the class.

Cyber Silent Stalker

Cyber Silent Stalker is a maze game that utilize lighting and stealth as the main gameplay mechanics. It was developed as a submission for the UCI Proto Game Jam in Fall 2018. My roles in this project include Designing, Programming, as well as creating and implementing VFX Effects.

PLAY

Trenchcoat Raccoons

I worked on Trenchcoat Raccoons as a team lead, game designer, programmer, artist, and animator. The game was completed as part of the UCI Video Game Development Club's Fall Quarter 2018 Project. We wanted to do something absurd, thus we set the win condition to stacking your raccoons high enough to shoot the sun in the face. The art assets and animation were made by me quite late into development, when we discovered that the club's art team would not be providing asset in a timely manner.

PLAY

Wildfires at Night

Wildfires at Night, built in Q1 2019, is an unintended product of my experimentation with light as a core mechanic. After developing Cyber Silent Stalker's graphic, I wanted to push the concept further and make a minimalistic game where vision is a resource. Ultimately the prototype turns out to be a generic horde shooter, but nevertheless I am quite satisfied with the resulting visual and atmosphere.
